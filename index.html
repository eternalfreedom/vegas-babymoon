♡ Babymoon Essentials
♡
Avoid casino smoke: Use the Palazzo tower elevator and Grand Canal Shoppes path — bypasses the casino floor entirely. Ask hotel staff for the tower route on arrival.
♡
Hydration: Vegas air is ~5% humidity — far drier than Toronto. Pregnancy increases fluid needs. Large water bottle everywhere, especially in the rental car.
♡
June heat: 100–105°F by midday. All outdoor activity before 10am or after 6pm. Rideshare freely — never feel bad about a Lyft in June heat while pregnant.
♡
Soaking tub: Palazzo suite has a deep tub. Use it every evening — great for leg and back relief over 5 nights.
♡
Nearest hospital: Sunrise Hospital & Medical Center — 3186 S Maryland Pkwy, ~10 min from Strip. Save the address. Just smart to have.
💡 Things Most People Miss
💡
Mott 32 Peking duck: Must be pre-ordered 24 hrs ahead when booking. Carved tableside. The best Peking duck in Las Vegas — don't skip it.
💡
Shark Reef jellyfish room: Most visitors rush through it. The jellyfish exhibit is lit beautifully — spend 15 min just standing and watching. Genuinely hypnotic.
💡
Mob Museum speakeasy: The Underground is in the basement — Prohibition-era bar with real craft mocktails. Worth 20 min after the exhibits.
💡
Bellagio bridge: The footbridge spanning the lake gives the straight-on view of the entire fountain spread. Arrive 5 min early on the 8pm Bocelli show — it draws a crowd on Saturdays.
💡
DTF is at ARIA, not Venetian: 3730 Las Vegas Blvd S (ARIA Resort). A short Lyft or 12-min walk south from the Palazzo. Don't show up at the Venetian looking for it.
